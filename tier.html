TIERRAPOND - IONIC MINERALS & TRACE ELEMENTS

A Natural source of more than a 60 minerals & trace elements to increase the potential of the depleted pond soils and pond waters.

Improves primary phyto and zoo plankton production in the water by remineralizing with a natural catalyst of mineral & trace elements.

Composition

Hydrated Calcium Alumino - Silicate hydroxide with many other elements including potassium, iron, magnesium, phosphorus, manganese and over 50 more mineral elements.

Formula :

Al2[(OH)2|Si4O10].nH2O

Indication for use :

Removes toxins, Pathogens, bad bacteria and promotes the health of aquatic animal.

Application :

Soil treatment - 50-100kgs/ha

Pond water :

20-45kgs/ha in 1 Meter water level once in 7 to 10 days optimum and healthier algal bloom in water.
