About the Company!

DB BIO ORGANICS is a provider of environment friendly, aquafriendly aquaculture products.

A group of highly qualified technical and business experts come together to provide a niche combination of environmental engineering services and aquaculture improving products that seek to address the critical issues pertaining to growth, yield goodhealth, disinfection and water treatment.

The company headquarters and manufacturing base in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The factory is capable of manufacturing over 100000kg/litre per annum across 3 shifts.

The concepts of DB BIO ORGANICS was conceived in 2019. Considerable time hasbeen spent in understanding the markets, developing products, conducting pilotprojects trials based on opportunity & verticals identified in the environmental space.
