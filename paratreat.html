EBI PARATREAT

EBI PARATREAT is one of the most broadly discussed shrimp disease recently. The EHP reproductive and the spreading rate are very fast. Our EBI PARATREAT EHP control liquid solution reduce or kill the free spores outside the cell. It is made from high unit of Vitamin B group, probiotics, metabolites and taurine that can functions of the digestion system and help microvilli grow and also repair the heptopancreas from the EHP damages.

How to use:

a) 1 ltr or 2 ltr (Depends upon bio burden in pond) mix with 100 ltr of water sprayed on 1 acre pond.
b) After 24 hrs. sprayed a water probiotics on the same pond
c) Observe for 10 to 14 days there is no any symptoms of EHP. If you found any symptoms of EHP apply same dose as per section a.

Ingredients :

Bacillus spps., serum, Sacchromyces, Vitamin C, Pepsi, lactobacillus species, blend of multiple organic acids, blend of digestive enzymes.

Storage :

Cool & dry place, keep out of children. Protect from direct sunlight.
